Who we are...

We are a company committed with you to help you reach your goals.
HAW is a Mexican company that provides you with all you need to eat healthy while you keep it up at work, with your family or studying. The process starts with a brief visit to one of our renown nutritionist. Which is going to create a food plan just for you, so you have the energy you need in the healthies way possible. With your eating plan, all you have to do is log in, decide if you want to eat at home, your office or one of our many restaurants all over the city.

Mission

Mexico is one of the most obese countries in the world. Our mission is to help people who have no time to get a healthy diet. Thus, we want to reduce our people's healthy issues, help them to feel better, confident and also to look good.

Vision

HAW is a company that pretends to become international, to help people all around the globe. We also want to help nutrition to be balanced. To bring food where there is none and the other way around.
